HOW TO PLAY

Trade volatile digital assets across global tech hubs. Pay off your debt and build your fortune before time runs out.

BASICS

  • Buy low, sell high -- prices vary between cities
  • Travel between cities (costs 1 day + airfare based on distance)
  • Click asset names to see cross-city price comparison
  • Pay off your debt -- interest compounds daily!

MISSIONS

  • Accept side missions for bonus cash rewards
  • Deliver assets to specific cities, hit price targets, or collect sets
  • Up to 2 active missions at once

PERKS

  • Buy permanent upgrades from the Perk Shop
  • Reduce buy prices, increase storage, lower heat, and more

ENCOUNTERS

  • Random choice events with risk/reward decisions
  • Shady deals, mysterious strangers, and opportunities

HEAT & CONTRABAND

  • Trading ZeroDayExploits raises your heat level
  • High heat = feds can raid you
  • Buy insurance or the VPN Shield perk for protection

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

  • T Travel | W Wait | L Loan Shark | P Perks | Esc Close
